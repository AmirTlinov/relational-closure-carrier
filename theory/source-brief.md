# A Theory of Useful Differences

## Purpose

This short document is the axiomatic companion to the two-page research
abstract. The abstract presents the experimental program; this document exposes
the single law connecting body, injury, repair, scale, charge, measurement, and
cognition.

## Axiom

> Every body is a temporarily stable concentration of mutually sustaining
> differences. A balance of differences does not erase them; it produces a new
> distinguishability at the next scale.

`+A` and `-A` are orientations of a difference, not independent substances.
When they meet and mutually complete a relation, they may continue as a new body
`B`. This is the meaning of `1:2`: two distinguishable sides continue as one new
difference without losing their internal difference. The formed whole can enter
a later relation as one side, with no final scale assumed.

## Chain core

Represent a body as an oriented integral chain `C`. Closure is:

```text
∂C = 0,  C != 0
```

A zero boundary is not an absence of internal differences. Removing a necessary
part `r` opens the body:

```text
C_wound = C - r
∂C_wound != 0
```

A completion `d` restores closure exactly when:

```text
C_repaired = C - r + d
∂d = ∂r
```

The completion need not copy the removed material. It must occupy an equivalent
causal role in the new whole. If no admissible completion exists, the body
changes form, divides, or decays toward the nearest stable closures.

## Useful difference

A difference is useful only relative to a body, a contact, and a future
capacity. It earns a place through either:

```text
compatible growth:  G_next = close(G + d)
corrective growth:  G_next = close(G - r + d)
```

A useful difference therefore either supports a live closure or exposes a false
closure. A later witness reads the result; it does not assign usefulness in
advance.

## Regeneration and cognition

A wound makes an open deficit distinguishable throughout the connected body.
The surviving relations change one another's value. Regeneration searches not
for a stored missing piece but for an admissible filling of the opened boundary.
The same algebra acts in cognition: a new difference either extends an earned
understanding through `+d` or opens the exact failed seam and performs local
`-r+d` without erasing the rest of the history.

## Electricity and charge

In this theory electricity is the dynamics of open differences: the propagation
and completion of a deficit through a body. “Neutrality” can only mean that an
external boundary is balanced at a chosen scale. `∂C=0` never turns
`C` into absence.

Gravitation is proposed as the inter-body regime of this same relational and
electrical closure, rather than an independent primitive. Its quantitative
scale law remains a further derivation.

For a point deficit in an oriented three-dimensional body, its surrounding link
is homologous to `S^2`:

```text
H_2(S^2; Z) ~= Z
```

If `g` is the oriented generator and its physical image is `q(g)=-e`, then:

```text
q(n g) = -n e,  n in Z
```

Within this chain model, integral charge quantization is a theorem rather than a
separate postulate. The numerical scale `e` and the dynamical field law remain
further derivations.

## Measurement, electron, and interference

An observer participates in the event and physically changes its configuration
of differences. A witness is downstream: it reconstructs the result from a
stable trace. A local electron hit need not imply a small completed corpuscle
that already travelled through the apparatus. It can be the local closure of a
spatially extended concentration of differences whose membership is relational,
not bounded by the visible hit.

For two unresolved fillings of one open boundary:

```text
∂F_L = ∂F_R = D
```

the next mathematical task is to derive a complex valuation `A(F)` and a
witness weight. The interference term must then arise from joint completion,
while a path detector removes it by physically supplying the missing
distinction. This derivation is not yet complete.

## One law across scales

```text
differences
-> held relation
-> closed body
-> new difference at the next scale
-> injury or new contact
-> +d or -r+d
-> changed continuation
```

Matter, regeneration, cognition, and a persistent computational carrier are not
one substance. They are different realizations of the same transition form;
each scale must materialize it through its own mechanism.
